About Michael

Mechanical, electrical, and mechatronics engineer

Hi! I grew up in southwest Virginia, deep in the Appalachian Mountains. In 2019, I graduated with an undergraduate degree in Electrical Engineering from Cornell University. My expertise lies within the interesection of mechanical and electrical engineering and I am always looking to learn new skills.

Previously, I've run a maker space, taught CAD courses for Autodesk, worked with startups at a hardware acclerator, and designed electro-mechanical products at a product development consulting company. Currently, I am working as a mechatronics engineer at Berkshire Grey, a robotics company pushing the boundaries of automation in e-commerce, retail replenishment, and logistics.

Outside of work, I am an avid rock climber, focusing on outdoor bouldering and sport climbing. Check out some of my favorite climbs here! I also enjoy cooking, backpacking, board games and digital art.
