A modular wine tumbler

Facture Product Development

Overview

During my time at Facture Product Development, a cilent tasked us with designing a modular wine tumbler. Unlike other wine tublers, this product would have a mechanism in the base to attach various extensions to the tumbler. Some of these attachments include a wine stem, mug handle, ground stake, and handle clamp. In this project, I primarily worked as an industrial designer - producing renders, creating production drawings, and helping with the mechanical design as well.
