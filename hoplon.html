Lasercut Book Cover

Personal Project

This project was a practical exploration of lasercut living hinges. After experimenting with different designs and dimensions, I was able to pinpoin the most effective living hinge with the necessary flexibility while balancing the amount of time it took to print. The product itself features just five separate pieces, joined with wood glue at the fingers. An elastic is added at the end to keep the front of the cover secure. A storage slot is also included to store pens, notes, or any other handy stationary tools.
