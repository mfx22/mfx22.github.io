An automated glassware cleaner finished product

Facture Product Development

Overview

This was a continuation of this project during my time at Facture Product Development. The product itself is designed to efficiently and effectively clean glassware products, requiring as little effort from the user as possible. Fortunately, our past work developing this process was done well so this portion of the project focused more on transitioning the concept into a more polished product with thought going into manufacturability and sourcing. On this project, I worked as a secondary mechanical engineer and the industrial engineer. This meant, I was responsible for designing parts, creating manufacturing drawings, sourcing components, communicating with vendors, and exploring color, material, and finish through renders.
